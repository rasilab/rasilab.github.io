    Tissue culture jobs

    • Updated by Jamie Yelland, 2025-01-06. This checklist covers the chores and ordering required to maintain the TC lab.

    Equipment checklist¶

    Vacuum Traps¶

    1. If the liquid in the trap flask is over the fill line, add 100mL of 100% bleach, allow to sit for ≥1 hour. Solution should turn blue, then clear. Once the solution is clear, dump down the sink while flushing the sink with running water. Add 100mL of 100% bleach to the empty flask and replace it back in the vacuum line.
    2. Autoclave the waste collection flasks approximately every two months.

    Biohazard waste¶

    1. Dispose of biohazard when it is ≥75% full. Check weekly.
    2. Waste can be brought down to the biohazard disposal room, on floor E. Bags should be labeled with room number "B3-135", "Subramaniam", and the date.

    Incubators¶

    1. Replace the water in the incubator every 4 weeks.
    2. To do this, mix 2 L of autoclaved H2O with 2-4 mL of LabCare incubator water disinfectant solution to prevent contaminants from growing.
    3. Spray the water tray with 70% ethanol and allow to dry before adding fresh water.

    Dry baths¶

    1. Sterilize the dry bath pellets every 6-12 months by autoclaving.

    Ethanol¶

    1. If empty, replace ethanol in the 5 gallon drum below the sink.
    2. To do this, bring the tank down to the glassware services center on floor D. They have a cupboard for ethanol tank refill. Leave the tank in there with a purchase form (available by the cupboard). Purchase form just needs to say "vendor part# = EtOH, quantity = 5 gallons, 95% ethanol (5-gallon can)" plus our room number and a PO#, and they'll figure it out. The filled can will be returned within a couple of days.
    3. Use project # 228808 (last updated 10-09-23)

    Have/Need? Reagents Minimum quantity
    100% FBS 3 frozen 50mL aliquots
    Puromycin (2mg/mL) 5 x 1mL aliquots
    Blasticidin (10mg/mL) 5 x 1mL aliquots
    Hygromycin (100mg/mL) 5 x 1mL aliquots
    Doxicyline (20 mg/mL) 5 x 50uL aliquots
    AP1903 5 x 50uL aliquots
    • Note: We should have 5+ 500mL bottles of FBS stored at -80C.
    • When the -20C 50mL FBS aliqouts start running low, thaw one of these bottles overnight at 4C in the TC fridge (might take more than one night to thaw).
    • Once thawed, aliquot ~47.5mL of this into 10-11 50mL conical tubes, to create new "50mL" aliquots. The reason we don't fill all the way to 50 mL is that the FBS expands when it refreezes, and can crack the tubes if there's not enough space.
    • It's okay to have a bit less than 50mL added to each DMEM bottle (47.5mL into 500mL DMEM =~ 9% FBS, which is fine for our lines).
    • See Make 2mg/mL Blasticidin protocol for general instructions for making human antibiotic stocks. (Note: we now make 10mg/mL blasticidin aliquots; just adjust the protocol volumes for your desired antibiotic concentration).
