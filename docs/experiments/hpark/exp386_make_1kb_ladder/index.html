    Title: Exp 386 Make Home-made 1kb Ladder¶

    Date: "2021-08-26"¶

    Goal¶

    Make Home-made 1kb Ladder

    Background¶

    We have pPSU1 (1kb/100bp ladder construct) and pPSU2 (1kb/100bp ladder construct) from Addgene. By using these two plasmids, we can make the home-made 1kb ladder fro the lab use.

    Solution¶

    Strategy¶

    1. I could not find the plasmids or glycerol stocks of pPSU1/2.
    2. I found the original bottles from Addgene.
    3. I picked cells from these old addgene bottles to grow but nothing grew.
    4. Add 1ml of P1 buffer into each bottle then perform miniprep.
    5. Perform electroporation of the extracted pPSU1/2 into the home-made electrocompetent cells.
    6. Recover cells in 1ml LB for 1 hour.
    7. Spin down recovered cells in 2 minutes and 9k rpm to concentrate.
    8. Remove ~ 900ul of supernatant.
    9. Resuspend cells in 100 ul of the remained LB then plate whole volume onto LB+Carb plate.
    10. Incubate them at 37C for overnight.
    11. Scrap all colonies and make glycerol stock and midiprep them.

    Procedure¶

    Glyerol stock and plasmid extration¶

    DONE [2021-08-25 Wed] Grow E.coli cells of pPSU1/2¶

    1. Found the old pPSU1/2 bottles from Addgene.
    2. Pick cells from the original bottles from Addgene.
    3. Grow them in 5ml of LB+carb at 37C, 200rpm for overnight.
    4. Nothing grew.
    5. All cells died?

    DONE [2021-08-27 Fri] Extract directly from the pPSU1/2 Addgene bottles.¶

    1. [2021-08-26 Thu] Add 1ml of P1 buffer into pPSU1/2 Addgene bottle and incubate them in the rotator at RoomT for overnight.
    2. [2021-08-27 Fri] Take out the supernatant ~750ul and put them in the conical tubes.
    3. Add the same volume (~750ul) of P2 buffer then gently mix.
    4. Add the same volume of P3 buffer (~1.5 ml) then mix well.
    5. Divide them into 3x 1ml into 1.5 eppitubes.
    6. Miniprep them and elute them in 30ul.
    7. Concentrations of plasmids
    8. pPSU1 = 45 ng/ul
    9. pPSU2 = 89 ng/ul

    DONE [2021-08-27 Fri] Electroporation pPSU1/2 into the home-made electrocompetent cells.¶

    1. Add 1 ul of each extarcted plasmid into the thawed 50ul of home-mede electrocompetent cells in ice.
    2. Perform expectroporation: Both have 5.5ms
    3. Recover them in 1ml of LB at 37C, 200rpm for 1 hour.
    4. Transfer them into 1.5 ml of eppitubes and spind down for 2 minutes, 9k rpm.
    5. Remover 900ul of supernatant.
    6. Resuspend cells in the remianed ~100ul LB then plate them all onto pre-warmed LB+carb plates.
    7. Incubate them at 37C for overnight.
    8. Results: Bothe have couple of thounsands colocnies

    DONE [2021-08-30 Mon] Glycerol stocks and plasmid extarction¶

    1. Scrap whole colonies in 10ml of LB.
    2. Mix cells well and make 15% glycerol stocks of NEB stable + pPSU1 and pPSU2.
    3. 2x 1ml glycerol stocks of pHPEC1 and pHPEC2 and save them in HPark E.coli (pHPEC) box1 at -80C.
    4. 10x 200ul glyerol and save them in HPark E.coly for 1kb ladder at -80C for the future plasmid extraction.
    5. Midiprep pPSU1 and pPSU2
    6. Label
    plasmid E.coli description parent Conc.
    pHPEC1 ecHPEC1 pPSU1 (500 bp EcoRV fragment) pUC9 280 ng/ul in ~400ul
    pHPEC2 ecHPEC2 pPSU2 (750 bp EcoRV fragment) pUC9 277 ng/ul in ~400ul

    Preparation of 1kb ladder¶

    DONE [2021-08-31 Tue] Screen pPSU1 (pHPEC1) and pPSU2 (pHPHSEC2) with various enzymes (EcoRI, EcoRV, BglII, PstI)¶

    1. Cut pHPEC1 with pHPEC1 with PstI, EcoR1, EcoRV, BglII

      • Divide MM into 4x 19.5 then add 0.5 ul of each enzyme (EcoRV/EcoRI/BglII/PstI).

    3. For pPSU2

      To make MM Volume (µl) for 20 µl reaction 3.5x
      Fast Digest enzyme 0.5 --
      10XFast Digest Green Buffer 2.0 7.0
      dd H₂O 14.5 50.75
      plasmid: pPSU2 3 µl (~0.8 µg) 10.5

    4. Divide MM into 3x 19.5 then add 0.5 ul of each enzyme (EcoRV/BglII/PstI).

    5. Digest it at 37 ℃ water bath for 2 hours
