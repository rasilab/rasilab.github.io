    Title: Exp422 Extract RT and TadA-XTEN48-antiGFP-nanobody¶

    Date: "2022-02-01"¶

    Goal¶

    Extract RT and TadA-XTEN48-antiGFP-nanobody

    Issue¶

    Extract RT and TadA-XTEN48-antiGFP-nanobody

    Strategy¶

    Procedure¶

    Extract RT/TadA-XTEN48-antiGFP-nanobody¶

    DONE [2022-02-24 Thu] Cultures¶

    1. Grow ecHPHS344-2/pHPHS536-1 in 5 ml of SuperBroth + 1xKan at 37C, 200rpm for overnight [4:25pm-12:50pm].
    2. In the morning dilute them in 50 fold into 250 ml of SuperBroth in 1l flask at 18C Strong lab shaker.
    3. After ~5 hours [12:50pm-5:50pm] add 300ul of 1M IPTG into each culture and grow them under the same coondition for ~18-19 hours [~18 hours].
    4. Spin down in 1L bottle 4000rpm, 25 min, 4C. Discard supernatant.
    5. Resuspend cell pellets in 1x 25ml of 1x lysis buffer.
    6. Label:
    7. 1: RT (pHPHS344-2)
    8. 2: TadA-XTEN48-antiGFP-nanobody (pHPHS536-1)

    Prepare protein samples for purification: I will use I made before keeping at 4C.¶

    1. Make 2X Lysis/Elusion buffer:
    Final Concentration M.W / Stock solution
    50 mM Tris/HCl pH 7.5 50 ml 20ml 1M Tris pH 7.5
    300 mM KCl 22.38 g g 74.6
    Water to final volume 500 ml 200ml
    1. Make 2X storage buffer:
    Final Concentration M.W / Stock solution
    50 mM Tris/HCl pH 7.5 50 ml 20ml 1M Tris pH 7.5
    100 mM KCl 7.5 g g 74.6
    Water to final volume 500 ml 200ml

    • Note: Before stroing samples add 1mM DTT.

    • Make lysis buffer: Make 200 ml

    Final Concentration 500 ml M.W / Stock solution
    2X stock 250 ml 2x
    20 mM Imidazole 0.68 g 68
    1 mM DTT 500 ul 1 M (154 mg in 1 ml of dH2O)
    1 mM PMSF 500 ul 1 M (174 mg in 1ml of DMSO)
    H2O to 500 ml
    1. Make elution buffer: make 50 ml
    Elution buffer 50ml Molecular weight
    2x stock 25ml
    500mM Imidazole 1.7 g 68
    H2O To 50ml
    1. Thaw frozen pellets from -80C in iced water for 30 minutes.
    2. We directly spin down cell cultures. So skip this step.
    3. Spin down cells in 4000rpm for 25 minutes.
    4. Dump supernatants.
    5. Resuspend cell pellets in 15ml of lysis buffer.
    6. Put cells in a metal can and keep it in the small ice bath.
    7. Lyse by sonication (6-10x30s pulsing in 50ml conical in an ice-water bath, with 30s cooling in between, Macro-Tip at 100% amplitude, Sonifier in gel cutting room).
    8. Clean sonication tip by ddH2O before using new sample
    9. Transfer them in a new 50mL tubes (Lysate).
    10. Clear lysate by centrifugation for 45 min at 17,000g and 4ËšC in the new centrifuge in Roland's lab. We can directly load the lysate as 2x 15ml in 50ml regular Falcon tubes. Move the supernatant to a fresh 50ml tube.
    11. Combined the supernatant.
      • We use one tube for each sample so don't need to combine.

    12. Get two binding tubes from Strong lab and stand. Mix well Ni2+-NTA from 4C and put ~2ml of them into each binidng tube. Flow trough the ethanol.

      • I use two binidng tube I bought.

    13. Then lysis buffer ~15ml into the column (Poly-Prep Chromatography Columns: Bio-Rad Cat# 731-1550) to wash beads.

    14. Close knob of the binding tubes and add little volume of 1x lysis buffer and transfer whole volume of beads into a new 50ml tubes and add supernatant of samples.
    15. Incubate them at 4C rotator for 30 minutes.
    16. Add lysates into the column and wait until all liquid is gone: Flowthrough (FD).
    17. Wash with three column volumes of lysis buffer (A1_2/B1_2/C1_2) before elution (E1_2) with 15ml of elution buffer with 500mM imidazole.
    18. Repeat this 3 times.
    19. Keep FT/A1_2/B1_2/c1_2/E1_2 at 4C.
    20. Keep small volumes (20ul in 4C) of lysate and flowtrhough from 1/2/3 wash steps.
      • I kept 30ul of each samples in the PCR tubes for Coomasie stain on [2022-02-28 Mon].

    DONE [2022-02-25 Fri] Concentrate extracted proteins¶

    1. Transfer E1/E2 into two new "Sartorius Vivaspin Turbo 15 Centrifugal Concentrators, MWCO: 30,000 (for RT) and 10,000 (for tadA)" and spin down them at 4000g for 10 minutes.
    2. Once 0.5ml of sample is remained in the top part, add 2x storage buffer ~15ml then spin down again at 4000g for 10 minutes.
    3. Repeat the above to exchange buffer.
    4. Add the same volume 100% glycerol and 1ul of 1M DTT into 1ml of samples.
    5. Aliquote 10x 100 ul and keep them at -80C except one at -20C. 6 Concentrations:
      • 1 (RT): 0.683 mg/ml (92.9k Da)
      • 2 (TadA): 0.053 mg/ml (55.2k Da)

    TODO [2022-02-28 Mon] Coomasie gel¶

    1. Add the same volum (20ul) of 2x sample buffer to the samples and heat them at 95C for 5 minutes.
    2. Run gel at 180V for 50 minutes.
    3. Stain with Commassie for ~60 minutes.
    4. Wash the gel by ddH2O for 1 hours. Change water every 5 minutes.
    5. Take images

    1. Label (Left to right): L1/FT1/A1/B1/C1/E1/Concentrated1(1/5 dilution), ladder, L2/FT2/A2/B2/C2/E2/Concentrated2
