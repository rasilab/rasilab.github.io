    Title: Exp369 Extarct Tev and His14-Tev-SENPEuB protease¶

    Date: "2021-07-08"¶

    Goal¶

    Extarct Tev and His14-Tev-SENPEuB protease

    Issue¶

    Extarct Tev and His14-Tev-SENPEuB protease

    Strategy¶

    Procedure¶

    Tev and His14-Tev-SENPEuB protease¶

    DONE [2021-07-06 Tue] Cultures¶

    1. Grow ecHPHS289 (NEB-Express Iq + pHPHS243: His14-Tev-SENPEuB protease) and ecHPHS346-1 in 6 ml of SuperBroth + 1xKan at 37C, 200rpm for overnight.
    2. In the morning dilute them in 50 fold into 300 ml of SuperBroth in 1l flask at 18C Strong lab shaker.
    3. After ~6 hours add 300ul of 1M IPTG into each culture and grow them under the same coondition for ~18-19 hours [4pm-11am].

    DONE [2021-06-18 Fri] Make Tris-HCl Stock Solution (1.0 M, pH 7.4)¶

    1. I use what I made on [2021-06-18]
    2. Sterile-filtered, if possible, and store in the refrigerator for up to 4 months.

    DONE [2021-07-08 Thu] Make PMSF and DTT 1Mstocks¶

    1. Make 1M PMSF: Add 174mg in 1ml (final volume) DMSO.
    2. Make 1M DTT: Add 154mg in 1ml (final volume) H2O.
    3. Note: Make 1 and 2 while I am spinning down cells. Save them at -20 C.

    DONE [2021-07-08 Thu] Grow Yev/Tev_protease strains¶

    1. I grew ecHP289 (label as "1") and ecHP346-1 (label as "2") in 300 ml of Super Broth in 1L of flask at 18C, 250rpm for overnight [~19 hours].
    2. Spin down in 2x 1L bottle 4000rpm, 25 min, 4C. Discard supernatant.
    3. Resuspend cell pellets in 2x 25ml of lysis+Sucrose buffer.
    4. Save cells -80C. Later thaw them in iced water for 30 minutes.

    1. Thaw frozen pellets from -80C in iced water for 30 minutes.
    2. We directly spin down cell cultures. So skip this step.
    3. Spin down cells in 4000rpm for 25 minutes.
    4. Dump supernatants.
    5. Resuspend cell pellets in 15ml of lysis buffer.
    6. Put cells in a metal can and keep it in the small ice bath.
    7. Lyse by sonication (6-10x30s pulsing in 50ml conical in an ice-water bath, with 30s cooling in between, Macro-Tip at 100% amplitude, Sonifier in gel cutting room).
    8. Transfer them in a new 50mL tubes (Lysate).
    9. Clear lysate by centrifugation for 45 min at 17,000g and 4˚C in the new centrifuge in Roland's lab. We can directly load the lysate as 2x 15ml in 50ml regular Falcon tubes. Move the supernatant to a fresh 50ml tube.
    10. Combined the supernatant.
    11. We use one yube for each sample so don't need to combine.
    12. Get two binding tubes from Strong lab and stand. Mix well with 2ml (total 6ml after well mix) Ni2+-NTA from 4C and put ~15ml of them into each binidng tube. Flow trough the ethanol.
    13. Then lysis buffer ~15ml into the column (Poly-Prep Chromatography Columns: Bio-Rad Cat# 731-1550) to wash beads.
    14. Close knob of the binding tubes and add little volume of 1x lysis buffer and transfer whole volume of beads into a new 50ml tubes and add supernatant of samples.
    15. Incubate them at 4C rotator for 30 minutes.
    16. Add lysates into the column and wait until all liquid is gone: Flowthrough (FD).
    17. Wash (A1_2/B1_2/C1_2) with three column volumes of lysis buffer before elution (E1_2) with 15ml of elution buffer with 500mM imidazole.
    18. Repeat this 3 times.
    19. Keep FT/A1_2/B1_2/C1_2/E1_2 at 4C.
    20. Keep small volumes (30ul in 4C) of flowtrhough (FT1_2) from 1/2/3 wash (A1_2/B1_2/C1_2) steps.
    21. Measure E1/E2 for A280

    TEV (E2) 0.51 44 0.87 0.58 13 1.5 ml (1.4 ml actual volume)

    • Combine 15ml of SENP (E1) and 1.5 ml of TEV (E2) in 30:1 ratio in a 50ml tube.

    • Rotate the combined samples in the rotator at RT for overnight.
    • Take 30ul of this E1+E2 sample after overnight and keep it at 4C with other samples.

    DONE [2021-07-09 Fri] Exchange/Concentrate extracted proteins¶

    1. Transfer E1+E2 and E2 into a new "Sartorius Vivaspin Turbo 15 Centrifugal Concentrators, MWCO: 50,000 d" and spin down them at 4000rpm for 10 minutes.
    2. Once 0.5ml of sample is reamined in the top part, add 2x storage buffer ~15ml then spin down again at 4000g for 10 minutes.
    3. Repeat the above to exchange buffer (total 2 times).
    4. For E2
    5. The top portion volume is anout 420ul.
    6. Add 420ul of 100% Glycerol and add 0.84 ul of 1M dTT to make 1mM of the final dTT concentration.
    7. Keep it at Rasi protein box at -20C until we aliquoting and transfer them to -80C.
    8. For E1+E2 (media exchanged to the storage buffer)
    9. After first wash by 2x storage buffer add ~16 ml iof 2x storage buffer.
    10. Transfer this into the used "1" Ni2+-NTA bead column and get the flowthrough to get the TEV which has cut HIS tag (this remained in beads)

    11. Take 30ul of sample and keep it at 4C for Coomasie stain.

    12. Add ~15 ml of 100% glycerol into ~15ml of E1+E2 mixture in RT for overnight. Add 30ul of 1M dTT to make 1mM final dTT concentration.

    13. Keep it at -20C Rasi protein box 1

    DONE [2021-07-12 Mon] Coomasie stain¶

    1. Prepare ~7 ul 2X Laemmli SDS-PAGE sample buffer in the PCR tubes
    2. Add 7 ul of samples from 4C.
    3. Boil them at 95℃, 5 min in PCR machine.
    4. Quickly spin in PCr tube spiner.
    5. Then load 10 ul of sample into the gel.

    DONE [2021-07-26 Mon] Concentrate SENP without His¶

    1. I have non-concentrated ~30ml of SENP without His from E1+E2 of e369 in 1x storage buffer + 50% glycerol with 1mM DTT.
    2. By using 10000kD concentrator concentrate 30ml of SENP without His.
    3. Spin down ~30ml of SENP without His from -20C Protein Box in 2x 10000kD concentror and spind down them at 4000rpm for hours.
    4. Repeat this until a few ml will be left in the top.
    5. Total 270 minutes spin then got ~7ml of concentrated sample.
    6. Measure A280 concentration and make 7x 1ml aliquotes.
    7. A280=0.566 mg/ml (before A280 = 0.159 mg/ml)
    8. Save them at -80C Rasi protein box.
    9. [2021-07-27 Tue] Concentrate again by using the same concentrator. ~3 more hours at 4000g at 4C to get 1.1ml in the top.

    10. A280 = 1.525 mg/ml

    11. Aliquote them in 11x 100ul and save them at -80C Rasi Protein box 1.

    12. Label as SENP_NoHis
