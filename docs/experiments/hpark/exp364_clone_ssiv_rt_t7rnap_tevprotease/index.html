    DONE [2021-06-30 Thu] Ecoli Chemical transformation.¶

    1. Perform [[http://rasilab.org/ln/hpark/2017/05/31/ecoli-chemical-transformation/][E.coli chemical transforamtion]]
    2. Prewarm 3x LB + Kan plates at 37 ℃.
    3. Thaw NEB-Express Iq chemically competent cells in ice for 10 minutes.
    4. Add 25 µl of NEB-Express Iq competent cells into 10 µl of gibson product.
    5. Keep them in ice for 30 minutes.
    6. Heat shock at 42 ℃ for 20 seconds.
    7. Keep cells in ice for 5 more minutes.
    8. Recover cells in 1 mL LB at 37 ℃, 200rpm for 60 minutes.
    9. Plate ~100 µl resuspended cells on prewarmed (for a couple of hours at 37 ℃) LB+ 1xKan
    10. Incubate them at 37 ℃ warm room for overnight
    11. Results
    sample ID Construct BB Insert 1 Insert 2 Insert 3 selection Number of colonies

    DONE [2021-07-06 tue] Coomasie strain¶

    1. Spin down culture in 3000g for 5 minutes then dump supernatant.
    2. I will use 1.7ml tube for spining. Put 1ml or 0.75 ml from each samples and spin down at 9000 rpm for 2 minutes.
    3. Resuspend pellet in ~100 ul 1X Laemmli SDS-PAGE sample buffer and boil at 95℃, 5 min.
    4. ~14,000g, 5 min spin. Transfer supernatant to a fresh 1.5ml tube. (Transferring can be skipped if you don’t see a visible pellet).
    5. Run 10 ul of each sample in a gel: 180V 50 min in new 1xSDS-PAGE buffer.
    6. Cut the gel and put it in the shake box.
    7. Coomasie stain for 1 hour.
    8. Destain with ddH2O for 1 hour.
