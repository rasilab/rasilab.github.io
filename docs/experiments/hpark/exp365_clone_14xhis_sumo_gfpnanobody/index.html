    DONE [2021-06-30 Thu] Ecoli Chemical transformation.¶

    1. Perform [[http://rasilab.org/ln/hpark/2017/05/31/ecoli-chemical-transformation/][E.coli chemical transforamtion]]
    2. Prewarm 3x LB + Carb plates at 37 ℃.
    3. Thaw chemically competent cells in ice for 10 minutes.
    4. Add 25 µl of competent cells into 10 µl of gibson product.
    5. Keep them in ice for 30 minutes.
    6. Heat shock at 42 ℃ for 45 seconds.
    7. Keep cells in ice for 3 more minutes.
    8. Recover cells in 1 mL LB at 37 ℃, 200rpm for 60 minutes.
    9. Plate ~100 µl resuspended cells on prewarmed (for a couple of hours at 37 ℃) LB+ 1xCarb
    10. Incubate them at 37 ℃ warm room for overnight
    11. Results
    sample ID BB Insert 1 selection Number of colonies
    365GBB1 365BB1 (2 ul) -- Amp 0
    365G1 365BB1 (2 ul) oAS1594 (1ul) Amp ~50
