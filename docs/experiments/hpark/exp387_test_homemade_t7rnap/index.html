    Title: Exp387 Test Home-made T7RNAP¶

    Date: "2021-04-13"¶

    Goal¶

    Test Home-made T7RNAP

    Issue¶

    Test Home-made T7RNAP

    Materials¶

    1. pHPHS309, oPB228, oAS1612 (template and oligos for PCRs to generate IVT template)
    2. Phusion PCR reagents
    3. Purified and commercial T7 RNAP
    4. T7 RNAP buffer (homemade; see recipe below)
    5. RNA cleanup buffers

    Buffers to make¶

    10X rxn buffer¶

    1. Make Tris pH = 7.9 -- Take Tris pH = 8 that I have, check pH, and if necessary, add HCl until pH = 7.9.
    2. Make 100 mM DTT in an eppy -- ratio is 15.4 mg to 1 mL H2O.
    3. Make 1 ml of 10X rxn buffer by mixing the following:
    Reagent 10X conc. 1X conc. Stock soln. conc. μl stock soln in 10X
    Tris pH = 7.9 400 mM 40 mM 1 M 400
    MgCl2 60 mM 6 mM 1 M 60
    DTT 10 mM 1 mM 100 mM 100
    Spermidine 20 mM 2 mM 100 mM 200
    H2O - - - 240
    1. pH 7.9 @ 25°C
    2. Make 10X version of above and store 10 x 100µl aliquots at -20C
    3. Patrick made the above 10x rnx buffer. I will use it.

    RNA cleanup buffer (10 ml)¶

    Reagent Conc. in buffer Stock soln. conc. amt. stock soln. in buffer
    GITC (for binding) 4 M use powder (118 g/mol) 4.72 g
    Tris pH = 7 25 mM 1 M 250 μl
    H2O - - to 10 ml
    1. Note: I will use the commercial RNA Clean and Concentrator-5 Kit

    RNA cleanup wash buffers (50 ml)¶

    1. Patrick essentially made the same buffer for column washes during RNA midipreps for ReLiC screens See here for buffer recipe.

    2. Pre-wash buffer

      Final Stock / F.W. Volume
      H2O - 30ml
      1M GuanidineHCl 95 m.w. 4.75g
      20 mM TrisCl pH 8 (DNA) or 7 (RNA) 1M Tris pH 8 1ml
      10% Ethanol 100% 5ml
      H2O - To 50ml

    3. Wash buffer

      Final Stock / F.W. Volume
      20 mM TrisCl pH 8 (DNA) or 7 (RNA) 1M Tris pH 8 1ml
      70% Ethanol 100% 35ml
      H2O - To 50ml

    Strategy¶

    1. Run reaction following NEB's protocol.
    2. Do 5 4x dilutions of the enzyme. Commercial as +ve control and no enzyme as -ve control.
    3. For template, PCR up from pHPHS309-1 using oPB228 and oAS1612 in 5x40µl reactions
    4. Use 500ng PCR template per reaction.
    5. Clean up RNA by mixing with the RNA cleanup buffer and washing with the RNA wash buffers here and Enzymax columns. Elute in 30µl of 10mM Tris pH 7.0.
    6. I decided to use RNA Clean & Concentrator-5 kit to clean IVT products.
    7. Measure concentration using Nanodrop

    DONE [2021-09-08 Wed] Test IVT rxns¶

    1. Set up 7 rxns:
    Rxn ID Rxn buffer T7 RNAP
    387R1 Homemade Homemade, neat
    387R2 Homemade Homemade, 1:4
    387R3 Homemade Homemade, 1:16
    387R4 Homemade Homemade, 1:64
    387R5 Homemade Homemade, 1:256
    387R6 Homemade Commercial
    387R7 Homemade Commercial and commercial 10xBuffer
    387R8 Homemade None (H2O)

    1. Make dilution series of homemade T7 RNAP:

      • Make 30 μl of 1X homemade buffer -- 3 μl of 10X buffer + 27 μl H2O.
      • Add 6 μl of 1X buffer to 4 tubes in a 5-tube strip (leave first one empty).
      • Add 6 μl of purified T7 RNAP prep to the first tube.
      • Transfer 2 μl from first tube to second tube. Mix. Repeat from second tube to third, etc.

    2. Set up rxn MM:

    Reagent μl/rxn μl in MM
    10X rxn buffer 2 18
    100 mM NTP 2 18
    387P1 template (~170 ng) 5 45
    H2O 9 81
    • Add Superase-In 2 ul into MM (forgot this)

    • Each reaction volume is 20 ul.

    • Aliquot 18 μl of this MM into 8 PCR tubes.

    • Add 2 μl of each T7 RNAP dilution to rxns as indicated in table above.
    • Incubate in thermocycler at 37 C for overnight (~18 hours).
    • Prepare 30ul of RN-H2O in 1.5 ml eppi-tubes.
    • Into each tubes with 30ul of H2O, add each 20ul reaction tube so the total volume will be 50ul.
    • Add 100ul of RNA binding buffer into each 50ul sample. Mix throughly.
    • Add 150 μl of 100 % EtOH into each 150ul sample tube.
    • Mix trhoughly and transfer into a Zymo-spin IC Column in a collection tube.
    • Centrifuge (room T at 16000g rpm for 30 seconds) and discard the flow-through.
    • Add 400 ul of RNA-Prep buffer to the column and centrifuge. Discard the flow-through.
    • Add 700 ul of RNA wash buffer to the column and centrifuge for 2 minutes to remove the remained ethanol.
    • Transfer the column carefully into the RNase-free tube.
    • Add 15 ul of DNase/RNase -free H2O directly into the column-matric and centrifuge.
    • Measure sample concentrations using nanodrop:
    Rxn ID T7 RNAP Concentration (ng/μl)
    387R1 Homemade, 1:1 1144
    387R2 Homemade, 1:4 1366
    387R3 Homemade, 1:16 1285
    387R4 Homemade, 1:64 1067
    387R5 Homemade, 1:256 475
    387R6 Commercial, 1:1 2181
    387R7 Commercial and commercial 10xBuffer, 1:1 2947
    387R8 None (H2O) 7
    1. Note: Make 1:50 fold diluted aliquotes as a working concentration.

    DONE [2021-09-08 Wed] Make a workig 1x T7RNAP stocks (diluted by 50 fold)¶

    1. Make 2X storage buffer:
    Final Concentration M.W / Stock solution
    50 mM Tris/HCl pH 7.5 50 ml 5ml 1M Tris pH 7.5
    100 mM KCl 7.5 g 750mg 74.6
    Water to final volume 500 ml 50ml
    1. Prepare add 1.25 ml of 100%Glycerol to 1.25ml of 2x storage buffer then mix well.
    2. Add 50 ul of T7RNAP stock (A280 = 5.698 mg/ml) thawed from -80C into this 2.5ml of storage buffer with 50% Glycerols.
    3. Add 2.55 ul of 1M DTT.
    4. Mix well and aliquote them into 24x 100ul with 1x T7RNAP 2021-09-08 labels.
    5. Save them in Rasi Working Protein Box1 at -80C.
