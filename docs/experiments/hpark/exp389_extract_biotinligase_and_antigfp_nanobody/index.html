    Title: Exp 389 Extract SUMOEu1-GFPnanobody and BirA biotin ligase¶

    Date: "2021-09-14"¶

    Goal¶

    Extract SUMOEu1-GFPnanobody and BirA biotin ligase

    Issue¶

    Extract SUMOEu1-GFPnanobody and BirA biotin ligase #68

    Background¶

    1. Rasi purified GFPnanobody and Biotin ligase previously, but lost it during cleanup after biotinylation.

    2. HPark also purifed GFPnanobody and Biotin ligase in the exp370 (Purify SUMOEu1-GFPnanobody and BirA biotin ligase #174) but due to the Protease contamination GFPnanobody were cut. Here I want to purify both proteins again to get more amount of intact ones.

    Strategy¶

    We follow same strategy as what we did for RT/APOBEC1 extraction in the Exp 381 Extract SSIV-RT and APOBEC1-antiGFP-nanobody

    1. Grow ecHPHS290 (NEB-Express Iq + pHPHS244 (pTP264): Biotin ligase BirA) and ecHPHS291 (NEB-Express Iq + pHPHS245 (pTP396): anti-GFP nanobody) in 6 ml of SuperBroth + 1xKan at 37C, 200rpm for overnight.
    2. In the morning, dilute them in 50 fold into 300 ml of SuperBroth in 1l flask at 18C Strong lab shaker.
    3. After ~6 hours add 300ul of 1M IPTG into each culture and grow them under the same coondition for ~18-19 hours.
    4. By using 2ml (total 4ml after well mix) new Ni2+-NTA, purify SSIV-RT and APOBEC1-anti-nanobody.
    5. Check them in Coomasie gel.
    6. Don't need FPLC
    7. Change buffer to the storage buffer and concentrate proteins by using a "Sartorius Vivaspin Turbo 15 Centrifugal Concentrators, MWCO: 30,000 d"
