    Strategy¶

    1. Cut base vector from #6 with BamHI.
    2. Run following PCRs:
    PCR # Name Fwd Rev Template DpnI Length Tm
    1 ADAR2-E488Q oAS1595 oAS1596 pASSC36 Yes 1257 58
    2 ADAR2-RESCUE oAS1597 oAS1598 pASSC37 Yes 1205 58
    1. 2-piece Gibson.
    2. Restriction check with XbaI and XhoI.
    3. Sequence with oAS1606 and oAS1607.
    4. Tranform into Gal inducibale Yeast strain (scAS19-1)

    Clone¶

    DONE [2021-07-06 Tue] Backbone¶

    1. Cut pHPHS347-1 with BamHI
    sample plasmid enzymes size
    367BB1 pHPHS347-1 BamHI "7279" bp
    1. MM
    To make MM Volume (µl) for 20 µl reaction 1x
    Fast Digest FastDigest BamHI 1.0 1.0

    dd H₂O 14.0 14.0

    DONE [2021-07-06 Tue] PCR inserts¶

    1. Do following PCRs:
    PCR # Name Fwd Rev Template DpnI Length Tm
    1 ADAR2-E488Q oAS1595 oAS1596 pASSC36 Yes 1257 58
    2 ADAR2-RESCUE oAS1597 oAS1598 pASSC37 Yes 1205 58
    1. Master mix
    Component 20 µl Reaction 2x Final Concentration
    Nuclease-free water to 20(so 12.1) µl 24.2
    5X Phusion HF or GC Buffer 4 µl 8.0
    10.0 mM dNTPs 0.4 µl 0.8
    10 µM Forward Primer: 1.0 µl -- 1.0 µM
    10 µM Reverse Primer: 1.0 µl -- 1.0 µM
    Template: plasmids (2ng/ul) 1.0 ul --
    Phusion DNA Polymerase (new homemade) 0.5 µl 1.0 --

    • Divide MM into 2x 17 ul then add 1ul of Fwd/Rev primers and plasmid templates

    • PCR conditions

    Step Temp Time
    Initial Denaturation 98°C 120 seconds
    30 Cycles Denaturation 98°C 5 seconds
    30 Cycles Annealing 58 ℃ 5 seconds
    30 Cycles 72°C 45 seconds
    Final Extension 72°C 5 minutes
    1. Run PCR.
    2. Run 2 µl of PCR samples in 1.2 % gel at 150V for 15 minutes.

    1. Results: Got expected bands. Also got very dim small extra bands from the PCR products.
    2. DpnI digestion. Add 1 ul of FD DpnI in 5ul of 10xFD buffer then add 2.5ul into 367P1/2 then incubate them at 37C water bath for 30 minutes.
    3. Clean them by the mini columns and elute them in 20 ul of RN-H2O.

    DONE [2021-07-07 Wed] Ecoli Chemical transformation.¶

    1. Perform [[http://rasilab.org/ln/hpark/2017/05/31/ecoli-chemical-transformation/][E.coli chemical transforamtion]]
    2. Prewarm 5x LB + Carb plates at 37 ℃.
    3. Thaw chemically competent cells in ice for 10 minutes.
    4. Add 25 µl of competent cells into 10 µl of gibson product.
    5. Keep them in ice for 30 minutes.
    6. Heat shock at 42 ℃ for 45 seconds.
    7. Keep cells in ice for 3 more minutes.
    8. Recover cells in 1 mL LB at 37 ℃, 200rpm for 60 minutes.
    9. Plate ~100 µl resuspended cells on prewarmed (for a couple of hours at 37 ℃) LB+ 1xCarb
    10. Incubate them at 37 ℃ warm room for overnight
    11. Results: Efficiency is very low for unkwon reason.

    Yeast transformation¶

    DONE [2021-07-15 Thu] Grow scAS19 (BCY123)¶

    1. Grow one colony from the scAS19 (BCY123) streaked plate on YEPD (incubated at 30C for 2 days and kept at 4C for ~10 days) in 3ml of YEPD at 30C, speed 8 roller drum for overnight.
    2. Dilute the overnight culture in 50ml of YEPD (2% Glucose) in 250ml of flask and grow them in the shaker at 30C, 200rpm for 4 more hours.

    DONE [2021-07-16 Fri] Yeast transformation¶

    1. Make transformation buffer
    Reagent and Stock Concentration Volume 10 reactions 3 reactions
    50% wt/vol PEG 3350 240μl 2.4ml 800 ul
    1M Lithium Acetate 36μl 360μl 120 ul
    2mg/ml Carrier DNA (5 min heat at 95C) 50μl 500μl 167 ul
    Total 326μl 3.26ml 1.09 ml
    1. Need 6x 10cm SCD-URA plates.

    2. I transformed the linearized plasmids following Standard yeast transformation protocol

    3. Add 100 µl of competent cells into 5 µl of each linearized DNA tubes + 15 ul of RN-H2O.

    4. Prepare 1x 100 µl cells without DNA as negative control.
    5. Heat shock for 40 minutes at 42 ℃ water bath.
    6. Plate 12 µl and ~110 ul cells on 10cm SCD-URA plates.
    7. Samples
    parent strain Plasmid plating volume

    scAS19 NA 110ul (1:1)
    scAS19 pHPHS350-2 110ul (1:1)
    scAS19 pHPHS351-1 110ul (1:1)
    1. Incubate the plate at 30 ℃ for 2 days.
