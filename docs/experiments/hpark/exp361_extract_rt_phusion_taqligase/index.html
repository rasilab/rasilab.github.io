    Title: Exp361 Extract RT/Phusion/Taq-Ligase¶

    Date: "2021-06-13"¶

    Goal¶

    Purify RT/Phusion/Taq-Ligase enzymes #45

    Issue¶

    Purify RT/Phusion/Taq-Ligase enzymes #45

    Strategy¶

    Small volume checking RT/Phusion/Taq-Ligase expression¶

    1. Grow 3 strains from the glycerol stocks in 2ml of Super-broth for overnight.
    2. ecHPHS294 (RT) in Rosetta-gami 2 strain.
    3. ecHPHS295_2_His14-Avi-SUMOEu1-Taq-ligase in NEB-Express Iq strain

    4. ecHPHS307_1/2/3_His14-Avi-SUMOEu1-Phusion in NEB-Express Iq strain

    5. Dilute ON culture in 5ml of Super-broth in OD600~0.2-0.3 and grow them again for 1:30 hour.

    6. Add 5ul of 1M IPTG (1mM final concentration) and grow for another 4 hours at 37C.
    7. Spin down culture in 3000g for 5 minutes then dump supernatant.
    8. Resuspend pellet in ~100 ul 1X Laemmli SDS-PAGE sample buffer and boil at 95℃, 5 min.
    9. ~14,000g, 5 min spin. Transfer supernatant to a fresh 1.5ml tube. (Transferring can be skipped if you don’t see a visible pellet).
    10. Run 10 ul of each sample in a gel: 180V 50 min in new 1xSDS-PAGE buffer.
    11. Cut the gel and put it in small black shake box.
    12. Coomasie stain for over night.
    13. Destain and image.

    Measuring Phusion activity¶

    1. Do 7×4-fold serial dilutions of 5µl purified Phusion into 15µl of the same storage buffer (without glycerol).
    2. Run following 20µl PCR reaction with 2µl undiluted Phusion and 7 serial dilutions above.
    PCR # Name Fwd Rev Template Length Tm
    1 GFPdropout + KanR oAS1411 oAS1315 pHPHS310 1755 52
    2 pU6-SV40pA oAS1406 oAS1571 pHPHS310 675 70
    Temp Time
    98C 2 min
    30 cycles of
    98C 2s
    52C 2s
    72C 1 min
    1. Run 2µl of each PCR on a 1% gel and identify the lowest concentration of Phusion that gives maximum product.
    2. Dilute Phusion to 2× the above concentration into storage buffer with 50% glycerol and aliquot it into 1ml aliquots in 1.5ml tube.
    3. Flash freeze in LN2 and store at -80C.
    4. For making working aliquots, thaw and aliquot 50µl into 1.5ml tubes.
    5. Use Phusion at 1µl per 20µl PCR reaction.

    Measuring Taq activity¶

    1. Do 7×4-fold serial dilutions of 5µl purified Taq into 15µl of the same storage buffer (without glycerol).
    2. Run following 20µl Gibson reaction with 2µl undiluted Taq and 7 serial dilutions above.
    Fragment # Name Concn / µl Vol
    1 KanR + ColE1 37ng 12µl
    2 pU6-SV40pA 32ng 4µl
    Volume materia;
    645μL H₂O
    320μL 5x Gibson Assembly Buffer

    1. Take 120µl of Gisbon Master Mix - Taq ligase.
    2. Mix 15µl of DNA.
    3. Mix 10µl of H2O.
    4. Divide into 8 × 18µl.
    5. Add 2µl undiluted Taq and 7 serial dilutions above.
    6. Do Gibson, 50C, 30 min.
    7. Run on gel and look for shift.
    8. Run 2µl of each PCR on a 1% gel and identify the lowest concentration of Phusion that gives maximum product.
    9. Dilute Phusion to 2× the above concentration into storage buffer with 50% glycerol and aliquot it into 1ml aliquots in 1.5ml tube.
    10. Flash freeze in LN2 and store at -80C.
    11. For making working aliquots, thaw and aliquot 50µl into 1.5ml tubes.
    12. Use Phusion at 1µl per 20µl PCR reaction.

    Phusion/Taq-Ligase extraction¶

    DONE [2021-06-18 Fri] Make Tris-HCl Stock Solution (1.0 M, pH 7.4)¶

    1. Material
    2. Tris Base (J.T. Baker, #X171)
    3. 12 M HCl Solution (Sigma-Aldrich, #H1758 – Note: Wear gloves, eye protection and exercise extreme caution when handling this strong acid solution. Aside from being dangerous, it also has a strong smell, so working under a fume hood is ideal.)
    4. Deionized water

    5. Normal lab equipment, such as pipettes, serological pipettes, graduated cylinders, beakers, a stir plate, a stir bar, a pH meter, a scale

    6. Procedure

    7. Add 60.57 g of Tris Base to an appropriate beaker (500 mL beaker in this case).

    8. Add 350 mL of deionized water to the beaker. Add a stir bar to the beaker and leave it on a stir plate until completely dissolved (~1 min).
    9. Begin monitoring pH of the solution. It should be basic (pH >7.4).
    10. Slowly add 32.5 mL of 12 M HCl, while monitoring the pH (Caution: wear gloves, eye protection and exercise extreme caution with this acid solution)
    11. Add more HCl as necessary until the pH of the solution reaches 7.4
    12. Once the pH of the solution is 7.4, add enough deionized water to raise the volume to 500 mL.
    13. Sterile-filter, if possible, and store in the refrigerator for up to 4 months.
