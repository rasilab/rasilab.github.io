    title: Exp353 Extract Cas9 and RT date: "2021-06-03"

    Goal¶

    Purify Cas9 and RT enzymes

    Issue¶

    https://github.com/rasilab/protocols_tutorials/issues/43

    Strategy¶

    Purification Protocol from Pleiner 2020¶

    From here.

    1. Transform pHPHS285/286 into Rosetta-gami 2 strains.
    2. Grow a 100 ml SuperBroth pre-culture overnight at 28˚C and dilute to 1 L after ~18 hours.
    3. After dilution, incubate culture at 18˚C until an OD600 of ~2.0 (2-4 hours).
    4. Induce expression by addition of 0.2 mM IPTG for 18-20 hours at 18˚C.
    5. Harvest cells and resuspend pellet in 120 ml lysis buffer (50 mM Tris/HCl pH 7.5, 300 mM NaCl, 20 mM imidazole, 1 mM DTT, 1 mM PMSF).
    6. Lyse by freeze-thaw followed by sonication (4x 1 min pulsing in thin-walled metal containers in an ice-water bath, Macro-Tip at 100% amplitude, Branson Sonifier).
    7. Clear lysate by centrifugation for 45 min at 17,000 rpm and 4˚C in an SS-34 rotor.
    8. Freeze half of the lysate after addition of 250 mM sucrose for later purifications.
    9. Incubate the other half (60 ml) in batch with ~4 ml bed volume Ni2+-NTA agarose (Thermo Fisher Scientific, USA) for 1 hour at 4˚C and then transfer to a gravity flow column.
    10. Wash with three column volumes of lysis buffer before stepwise elution with imidazole elution buffer (50 mM Tris/HCl pH 7.5, 300 mM NaCl, 500 mM imidazole, 1 mM DTT, 250 mM sucrose).
    11. Exchange eluate buffer with storage buffer (50 mM Tris/HCl pH 7.5, 200 mM NaCl, 1 mM DTT and 250 mM sucrose) using a PD-10 desalting column (GE Healthcare, USA).
    12. Aliquot purified protein, snap-freeze in liquid nitrogen, and stored at -80˚C until further use.

    2021-06-01¶

    • Scrape cells into ~30ml SuperBroth in 250ml flask. Grow them at 37C 200rpm for overnnight.
    • Make glycerol stocks from 1ml of resuspended cells.
    • Dilute to OD600 ~0.3 in 1L of super broth in 3l flask.
    • Grow for another ~5 hours at 18C , 250 rpm till you reach an OD600 of 0.44.
    • Grow ~2 more hours OD600 will be ~0.7
    • Add 1.0ml of 1M IPTG (1mM final concentration) and grow for another 18 hours at 18C.

    2021-06-02¶

    • Spin down in 2x 2x 1L bottle 4000rpm, 25 min, 4C. Discard supernatant.

    • Resuspend cell pellets in 2x 2x 15ml of lysis buffer. Then combine two each.

    • Add 35mg lysozyme in 1ml of H2O into each ~35ml resuspended cells.
      • Incubate them at 4C rotator for 30 minutes.
    • Lyse once by freeze-thaw in dry-ice ethanol. Followed by sonication (10x30s pulsing in 50ml conical in an ice-water bath, with 30s cooling in between, Macro-Tip at 100% amplitude, Sonifier in gel cutting room).
    • Clear lysate by centrifugation for 45 min at 17,000g and 4˚C in the new centrifuge in Roland's lab. We can directly load the lysate as 2x35ml in 50ml regular Falcon tubes. Move the supernatant to a fresh 50ml tube.
    • Put ~1 ml bed volume Ni2+-NTA agarose (total volume 3 ml) (Thermo Fisher Scientific, USA) in the diposable column and wait until all liquid is gone.
    • Then lysis buffer ~15ml into the column (Poly-Prep Chromatography Columns: Bio-Rad Cat# 731-1550) to wash beads.
    • Add lysates into the column and wait until all liquid is gone.

    • Keep small volumes of lystae and flowtrhough from 1/2/3 wash steps.
    • Run 7.5µl of each eluate in 4-20% Criterion gel, 180V, 50 min.
    • Concentrate the proteins to 0.5-1ml using Amicon Ultra 4 columns. Note that the Vivaspin PES columns are a cheaper alternate that work just as well.
    • Stain with Commassie for 4 hours.
