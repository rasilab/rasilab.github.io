We are looking for graduate students and postdoctoral fellows to join our lab. Research projects in our lab combine genomic and biochemical experimental approaches with computational modeling. We use mammalian cells and budding yeast as experimental models.

Postdoc candidates should email their CV, a cover letter describing their research interests, and contact information for two references. Graduate students at the University of Washington can arrange an in-person meeting to discuss possible rotation projects.
